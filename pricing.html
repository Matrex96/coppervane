The formula

How every estimate is built

Two steps: a national base price from the house itself, then a local adjustment from the ZIP code.

National = $850 + sqft × $4.25 + baths × $2,900
Local = National × ZIP index
Shown range = Local ± 15%
  1. Base fee — a fixed amount covering permits, setup and mobilization. Charged once per job.
  2. Rate per square foot — covers the rough plumbing that scales with the size of the house.
  3. Per bathroom — each full bathroom adds a fixed amount for its fixtures and tie-ins.
  4. ZIP index — the national price is multiplied by a local cost-of-labor index (e.g. San Francisco ×1.62, Plains ×0.99). Known metros match on the first 3 ZIP digits; everything else falls back to a broad region on the first digit.
  5. Range & rounding — the customer sees the local price plus a ± band, and all figures are rounded to the nearest $10.
