Why repipe your house?

It raises resale value

Buyers and inspectors flag aging pipes immediately. A documented repipe turns a negotiation point into a selling point.

It prevents expensive damage

Old galvanized and polybutylene lines fail without warning — and water damage is one of the costliest repairs a home can face.

Better water, every day

Steady pressure on every floor, no rust or discoloration, no metallic taste from corroded lines.

It pays along the way

No slow leaks quietly driving up the water bill — and many insurers offer better premiums on repiped homes.
